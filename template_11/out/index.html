Hello! I am a maid, and my mission is to create an atmosphere of impeccable cleanliness and comfort in your home. I pay special attention to every detail, so that your space becomes the perfect decoration in which you can relax and enjoy the comfort.

If you are looking for a reliable, professional and friendly maid, call me now! I will gladly accept the challenge and make your home cleaner, cozier and more pleasant to live in.

One-time cleaning
A one-time cleaning refers to a single session of deep cleaning or thorough tidying up of a space, such as a home, office, or any other area. It is typically performed on an irregular basis and is not part of a regular cleaning routine.
Twice a week
Cleaning "twice a week" refers to performing cleaning tasks or maintenance activities two times within a week. This frequency implies that the space is cleaned on a more regular basis, allowing for the upkeep of cleanliness and tidiness throughout the week.

Services
My services include such types of cleaning as: regular cleaning, window cleaning, after repairing, after relocation.You can learn more about them.
regular cleaning

I will regularly come and do comprehensive cleaning, including vacuuming, dusting, cleaning work and surface care. This will help you keep your space fresh and comfortable throughout the week.

window cleaning

I will professionally and carefully wash windows, wipe off dust, stains and dirt, so that you can enjoy the cleanliness and beautiful views from the windows.

after relocation

After the move, I will carefully clean and tidy up the new premises, remove dust, remove traces of the move and prepare it for your stay.

window cleaning

I will professionally and carefully wash windows, wipe off dust, stains and dirt, so that you can enjoy the cleanliness and beautiful views from the windows.
